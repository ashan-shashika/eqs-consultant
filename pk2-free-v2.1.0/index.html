About us


___  ___

EQS Consultants is a professional cost consulting firm that provides comprehensive construction cost estimating and cost planning from a project's inception thru closeout. In addition to cost estimating and cost planning, we provide related services that include quantity surveying, value engineering and cost management services. We have an unparalleled portfolio of projects encompasses many sectors of the construction industry. We have worked with many prominent architects, engineers and public agencies and have a reputation for providing high quality and dedicated service. Our project experience spans the full-breadth of the construction industry, including hospitality, healthcare, K-12 schools, higher education, aviation and transportation, cultural, commercial and institutional projects.
